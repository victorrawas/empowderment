Book a Call with me

Ready to discuss your beauty needs and how I can make your vision a reality? Booking a call is the first step to creating a personalized experience just for you. Whether you're a bride-to-be, a model, or a photographer looking to enhance your projects, I'm excited to connect with you and explore how I can elevate your unique style.

What to Expect:

During our call, we'll dive into the details of your upcoming event, photoshoot, or project. This is an opportunity for us to get to know each other, discuss your preferences, and tailor our services to meet your specific needs. Whether you have a clear vision in mind or are seeking professional guidance, I'm here to ensure the process is seamless and enjoyable.

How to Book:

Booking a call is easy! Simply follow these steps:

  1. Choose a date and time that suits your schedule from the calendar below.
  2. Add your contact info (phone/email) and any additional details you'd like to share.
  3. Book your time!

What You Need:

To make the most of our call, consider having the following information ready:

  • For Brides and Bridesmaids:

    • Wedding date and location
    • Preferred makeup style (e.g., natural, glamorous)
    • Any specific themes or inspirations

  • For Photographers and Models:

    • Details about the photoshoot or project
    • Preferred makeup looks or styles
    • Desired outcomes and expectations
